Khmer Digital is an official Khmer Unicode typeface developed by the Ministry of Post and Telecommunications of Cambodia (MPTC).

The font is designed to support modern digital communication, official government documents, and public-facing services, providing a clean and contemporary appearance while preserving the cultural integrity of Khmer typography.

Khmer Digital includes nine weights ranging from Thin to Black, offering flexibility for both display and body text across web, mobile, and print environments.

The typeface supports the full Khmer Unicode standard along with Latin characters, enabling consistent multilingual usage across digital platforms and enterprise systems.

Khmer Digital aims to serve as a national standard font for Cambodia, supporting digital transformation initiatives and improving the accessibility and readability of Khmer content.
